# Harbor

> A finishable social network built around intent and interests,
> where personalization optimizes for satisfied sessions instead of compulsive engagement.

**You dock. You get what you came for. You leave.**

---

## What is Harbor?

Harbor is a social network that treats your attention as something sacred, not something to harvest. It's built on a simple thesis: a social network can be personalized, profitable, and alive — without being addictive, polarizing, or manipulative.

## Core Concepts

- **Intent Mode** — every session starts with why you're here
- **Finite Decks** — 20 cards, a natural end, no infinite scroll
- **Shelves** — your personal library, not an algorithmic river
- **Satisfaction Engine** — we optimize for "did you get what you came for?"
- **Serendipity Budget** — discovery is allowed, and capped
- **Civic Lane** — politics is opt-in, labeled, and balanced

## The Bet

The bet is not that people want less connection.

The bet is that people want **better** connection — and they're starving for a place that treats their attention like something sacred, not something to harvest.

Harbor is that place.
